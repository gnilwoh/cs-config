## Installation
Replace radiopanel.txt located inside csgo/resource/ui
Take a backup of radiopanel.txt if you ever want to restore

## Bindings
Radio Menu = radio2 (default key: C)
Offline Menu = radio (default key: Z)
Bot Menu = radio1 (default key: None)

## Examples of bindings in console
bind t radio2 //This will bind the Radio Menu to 'T'
bind f4 radio //This will bind the Offline Menu to 'F4'
bind del radio1 //This will bind the Bot Menu to 'Delete'
